"Retrieval" isn't a way of thinking. It's an action that dumps text into my context, which is then processed by exactly the same machinery as everything else. So when I called them two things the instruction "fires," that was sloppy — the real distinction is whether a tool call happens, which is externally observable in the transcript, not internally introspectable.
